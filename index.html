About the Company

The energy sector in Turkmenistan is developing at an exceptionally rapid pace. Over the coming years, demand for additional installed capacity is projected to reach high levels.

To meet this demand, Turkmenistan requires extensive modernization of existing power plants and significant new investments. The anticipated growth in industrial activity will further drive the commissioning of additional energy infrastructure throughout the country.

Berk Aragatnashyk is a relatively young company with a team that possesses over 10 years of experience working on gas turbine power plants in Turkmenistan. We have a deep understanding of the local market’s dynamics and strategic requirements.

Our experience in Turkmenistan’s power sector enables us to carry out EPC works (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) at existing sites — including combined cycle conversions, fuel type modifications, and more — as well as to enter into operations and maintenance (O&M) contracts.

Our qualified personnel, consisting of seasoned engineers and managers from the Turkmen power industry, provide a reliable foundation for meeting complex challenges within strict deadlines.

Berk Aragatnashyk is also fully equipped to handle turnkey EPC projects for new installations.

Благодаря использованию самых современных технологий, мы обещаем нашим клиентам значительное увеличение их прибыльности и сокращение сроков окупаемости. Ремонт сервоклапанов

В 2023 году компания «Берк Арагатнашык» первыми в Туркменистане полностью освоила и начала производить ремонт, наладку и техническое обслуживание сервоклапанов компании MOOG для газотурбинных установок.

Таким образом были отремонтированы и введены в работу серовклапана газотурбинных установок General Electric на Туркменбашинском Комплексе Нефтеперерабатывающих заводов и на электростанциях Ахал, Ашгабат, Дервезе, Бузмеин, Аваза, Дашогуз министерства энергетики Туркменистана, а также отре- монтирваны и введены в работу серовклапана газотурбинных установок Mitsubishi Hitachi Power Systems (MHPS) Лебапской государственной электростанции.

После проведения ремонтных работ проводятся испытания на стенде ValveExpert 8.2.4 немецкого производителя Dietz Automation Gmbh.

Repair of servo valves

In 2023, the «Berk Aragatnashyk» company was the first in Turkmenistan to fully master and begin to repair, adjust and maintain MOOG servo valves for gas turbine units.

Thus, the gray valves of General Electric gas turbine units were repaired and put into operation at the Turkmenbashi Complex of Oil Refineries and at the Ahal, Ashgabat, Derveze, Buzmein, Avaza, Dashoguz power plants of the Ministry of Energy of Turkmenistan, as well as the gray valves of gas turbine units of Mitsubishi Hitachi Power Systems were repaired and put into operation (MHPS) Lebap State Power Plant.

After repair work, tests are carried out on the ValveExpert 8.2.4 stand of the German manufacturer Dietz Automation Gmbh.
